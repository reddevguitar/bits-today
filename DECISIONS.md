# DECISIONS

- Project title: `bit's today`
- Version 1 is a Bitcoin monitoring and decision-support agent
- The agent runs every 30 minutes
- It checks BTC status, relevant news, chart context, and notable public-figure investment news when fresh and relevant
- It outputs a human-readable Korean judgment and updates a paper-trading portfolio automatically
- The strategy should be more aggressive and short-term oriented than before, while remaining simulated only
- The portfolio may include BTC, ETH, other major coins, and selectively chosen high-volatility Upbit-listed small altcoins
- Small altcoins should not be pre-listed blindly; they should be added or removed dynamically based on market flow, news, and community attention
- The dashboard should show per-coin strategy notes for currently selected picks
- Initial virtual capital is 10,000,000 KRW
- Real exchange execution remains out of scope for now
