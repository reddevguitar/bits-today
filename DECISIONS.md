# DECISIONS

- Project title: `bit's today`
- The project is now Upbit-centered and altcoin-forward, not BTC-only
- Main cadence is now one autonomous strategy/improvement cycle every 6 hours
- The previous 30-minute lightweight execution model is deprecated
- The agent must maintain 5 preferred buy candidates at all times when data is available
- Preferred buy candidates should focus on Upbit-listed altcoins and exclude the top 4 largest Upbit majors
- Major coins may still be tracked as market context and risk barometers
- The strategy should be aggressive and diversified in paper trading, and should avoid defaulting to passive observation without a strong reason
- Candidate selection should compare turnover, momentum, community attention, news flow, and internal judgment
- Reports and dashboard output should become easier to scan, more visual, and more portal-like over time
- Each 6-hour cycle should include self-evaluation plus at least one concrete improvement to the strategy, report structure, or UI when feasible
- Real exchange execution remains out of scope for now
- External information gathering may use web search, web fetch, and local reasoning, but should stay selective rather than noisy
- In weak breadth sessions with major average range-position under roughly 20 and only 2-3 non-major all-positive 60%+ leaders, prefer a 70% all-positive support basket over defending mega-turnover salvage names.
- In a breadth-snapback session where major average range-position jumps from below roughly 20 to above roughly 80 within one cycle and 2개 이상 held breakout names collapse into clear trap territory, immediately abandon the old basket, raise paper deployment toward roughly 85%, and rebuild around current 85%+ leaders instead of defending the failed morning names.
- In a post-snapback failure cycle where major average range-position collapses by roughly 45 points or more, fresh breakouts fall from 3+ to 0, and 4개 이상 held preferred names lose roughly 45% day-range structure or break invalidation, force an immediate reset toward roughly 70%, keep at most 1 still-healthy quality hold, and rebuild around 5 all-positive survivor/continuation names instead of defending the failed rebound basket.
- In a breadth-rebound session where major average range-position reclaims roughly 60 or higher and multiple non-major leaders already hold 80%+ of the day range with real turnover, reject any same-day gainer that is still about 10% or more below its intraday high; promote cleaner liquid continuation leaders instead of chasing the largest candle.
- When `weak_breadth_warning` clears and top15 non-major low-range count improves by 5 or more within one cycle, do not wait for majors to turn green; immediately purge negative or sub-30% held names and rebuild around all-positive 70%+ leaders plus at most 1 mega-breakout core.
- When majors flip all-green and major average range-position rebounds above roughly 70 while fresh breakouts stay at 0, do not defend yesterday’s high-beta winners if 2개 이상 current holdings break invalidation or fall below roughly 50% of the day range; immediately rebuild around cleaner 60-90% continuation/survivor names and allow at most 1 fading high-beta slot with genuine multi-30B KRW turnover.
- When weak_breadth_warning turns true again while major average range-position still holds roughly 70 or higher, immediately evict any held name that slips to roughly 10% of the day range or lower, trim paper deployment toward roughly 80%, and refill with all-positive continuation/quality names instead of defending the older 85% basket.
- When a same-day mega-turnover leader is still up roughly 25% or more but collapses below roughly 60% of the day range with a day-high gap worse than roughly -15%, classify it as an exhausted blowoff and force the next cycle to rotate into cleaner 75%+ leaders instead of defending the headline volume.
- When weak_breadth_warning clears but 2개 이상 held fresh/beta names already break invalidation in the same cycle, do not average down into the old leaders; immediately reset into 5 all-positive 70%+ leaders where possible and cap the first re-risk step around roughly 85% until fresh-breakout breadth expands again.
- When an 85% all-positive basket is followed within one cycle by a major-average collapse from roughly the 60s to around 30 or lower and 3개 이상 held names slip below roughly 35% of the day range, force an immediate reset toward roughly 70%, keep at most 2 surviving green names, and refill the other slots with current 65%+ all-positive survivors plus at most 1 small clean high-range beta slot.
- When major average range-position rebounds above roughly 60 but weak_breadth_warning stays true and 2개 이상 held same-day leaders are already below roughly 45% of the day range or worse than roughly -15% day-high gap, force the next cycle to cap paper deployment around 80% and rebuild around all-positive 60%+ continuation/quality names plus at most 2 high-beta clean slots instead of defending the older turnover basket.
- When major average range-position falls back below roughly 35 while 3개 이상 current holdings are already below roughly 30% of the day range or break invalidation, force the next cycle to cut paper deployment toward roughly 75%, anchor exactly 1 fresh mega-liquidity leader if available, and rebuild the other 4 slots from cleaner positive survivor names instead of defending the broken prior basket.
- When major average range-position rebounds by roughly 50 points or more, weak_breadth_warning clears, and one or more held salvage cores still sit below roughly 55% of the day range, force a full reset into current 75-100% high-range leaders instead of defending the older salvage basket.
- When an 85% fresh-breakout basket is followed within one cycle by fresh breakouts dropping to 0 and 3개 이상 current holdings breaking invalidation, force an immediate reset toward roughly 80%, abandon the failed breakout names, and rebuild around liquid 65-100% survivor/reclaim leaders even if majors still hold in the 70s.
- When fresh breakouts stay at 0 while a held mega-turnover salvage core flips negative and sits roughly 10% or more below the intraday high, and at least 4 all-positive 60%+ alternatives exist, force that salvage slot out next cycle even if majors remain all-green; rebuild around current positive survivors instead of defending old size.
- When major average range-position rebounds toward roughly 75 or higher while a held high-beta slot slips below roughly 55% of the day range and two or more cleaner 75%+ liquid alternatives already exist, force that slot out next cycle even if price is still above the old invalidation level; upgrade into the cleaner continuation names instead of defending stale beta.
- When major average range-position falls below roughly 25 and only 1 or fewer current holdings remain all-positive while the rest lose their continuation structure, force the next cycle to cut paper deployment toward roughly 70% and rebuild around 5 current all-positive leaders instead of defending the broken basket.
